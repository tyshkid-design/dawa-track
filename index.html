Why DawaTrack

Built around the patient-doctor relationship

Every feature designed to close the gap between appointments and keep care continuous.

Medication Intelligence

Real-time dose logging with pattern detection. Patients log every dose; doctors see the full picture instantly.

Live Care Feed

Doctors send typed notes — praise, reminders, urgent alerts, clinical advice — directly to the patient's dashboard in real time.

Adherence Analytics

7-day trend charts, risk flags, and per-patient adherence rates give clinicians everything they need at a glance.

Role-Based Access

Patients see only their data. Doctors see their full patient panel. No crossover, no confusion, no privacy risk.

Instant Risk Alerts

When adherence drops below threshold, doctors are flagged immediately — enabling proactive intervention.

EHR-Ready Integration

Plug into existing hospital systems via HL7/FHIR standards. Minimal disruption, maximum value from day one.
