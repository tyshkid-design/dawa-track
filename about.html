Our Story

Built by people who care
about healthcare

DawaTrack was born in Nairobi from one frustration — patients weren't taking their medications, and doctors had no visibility into why. We built the bridge.

Our Mission

Close the gap between appointments

Medication non-adherence costs healthcare systems billions annually — and costs patients their health. DawaTrack exists to close that gap with technology that's simple enough for any patient and powerful enough for any clinician.

We empower hospitals with real-time adherence data, a direct care feed from doctor to patient, and analytics that transform how care teams operate between appointments.

What Drives Us

Our core values

Patient First

Every feature we build is evaluated by a single question: does this make the patient's life better and healthier?
