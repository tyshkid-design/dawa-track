Our Story

Built by people who care
about healthcare

DawaTrack was born from a simple frustration — patients weren't taking their medications, and doctors had no visibility into why. We set out to fix that.

Our Mission

Bridging the gap between patients and providers

Medication non-adherence costs healthcare systems billions every year and, more importantly, costs patients their health. DawaTrack exists to close that gap — with technology that's simple enough for patients and powerful enough for clinicians.

We empower hospitals with real-time data, smart analytics, and communication tools that transform how care teams and patients interact between appointments.
