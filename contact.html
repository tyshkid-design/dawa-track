FAQ

Common questions

How long does onboarding take?

Most hospitals are fully onboarded within 3–5 business days. Enterprise integrations with EHR systems take 2–4 weeks depending on the system.

Is patient data secure?

Yes. All data is end-to-end encrypted, hosted on HIPAA-compliant infrastructure, and access is strictly role-based. We maintain full audit logs.

Do patients need a smartphone?

No. DawaTrack is web-based and works on any device with a browser — including basic smartphones. SMS logging options are available for patients without data access.

Can we try it before committing?

Absolutely. Our Starter tier is free for up to 50 patients — no credit card required. You can also request a personalized demo from our team.
